Biography

I am an Associate Professor in the School of Computing at the University of Otago and an Honorary Associate Professor in the School of Computing at the Australian National University (ANU). I received the PhD degree in Computer Science from the University of Adelaide and did the Postdoc at the Royal Institute of Technology (KTH) in Sweden.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in parallel computing, distributed computing, wireless networking, and applied machine learning. My current research particularly focuses on

  • Photonic deep learning acceleration (photonic computing, photonic-interconnect of GPUs)
  • Photonic communication (optical-network-on-chips, routing and wavelength assignment)
  • Scheduling and optimisation (multi-core task scheduling, wireless link scheduling, UAV trajectory scheduling)
  • Machine learning and applications (federated learning, explainable learning)
  • Wireless networking (sensor networks, Internet-of-Things, 5G networks)
