I am an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at UNSW Sydney and an Honorary Associate Professor in the School of Computing at the Australian National University (ANU). I am broadly interested in parallel computing, distributed computing, wireless networking, and applied machine learning. My current research particularly focuses on

  • FPGA-based hardware acceleration (reconfigurable architectures for deep learning, heterogeneous FPGA + CPU/GPU co-design)
  • Photonic deep learning acceleration (photonic computing, photonic-interconnect of GPUs, optical-network-on-chips)
  • Machine learning and applications (federated learning, explainable learning, AI-enabled smart health)
  • Wireless networking (Low-power long-range wireless networks, Internet-of-Things, 5G networks)

I am looking for self-motivated PhD students to work on photonic computing and communication, machine learning acceleration, distributed learning, and wireless networking.
